‭(Hidden)‬ Special Announcement - SVSU Campus Closure/Class Cancelation Policies due to Weather or Other Adverse Conditions
As the winter weather is now upon us, please take a moment to review the Campus Closure Policy and Information.
 
SVSU Main Campus Closure/Class Cancelation Policy
SVSU Off-Campus Class Cancelation Policy

In the event of any severe weather or campus delays or closures, SVSU will communicate through an emergency notification system which delivers messages to SVSU email accounts, personal email addresses, land lines and mobile phones. 
 
Individuals may register up to:
• Two (2) additional email accounts
• Three (3) mobile phone numbers (NOTE:  Mobile service plans may charge for the delivery of text messages.)
• Three (3) voice only phone numbers 
Log-in to this new system www.getrave.com/login/svsu with your SVSU user name and password to verify that information in the system is correct and to make any necessary changes or additions to your contact information.
 
Questions about registering for the emergency notification system can be directed to the I.T. Support Center at (989) 964-4225 or support@svsu.edu.
